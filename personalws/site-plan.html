Overview

Purpose

To provide a safe, comfortable, and engaging environment where cats, dogs, and bunnies can relax and be cared for while their owners are away. We aim to deliver personalized pet care with love, professionalism, and tailored experiences for each animal's unique needs.

Audience

Pet owners (cats, dogs, and bunnies) looking for reliable boarding services during vacations, work trips, or emergencies. Individuals seeking daycare services for pets, luxury accommodations, or specialized care for older or special needs animals.

Dynamic elements

Pet Profile Section, Booking System, Live Webcam Feeds, Customer Reviews, Interactive Facility Tour, Blog Section

Content

Home page

Page 1: Home Page Header: Logo, navigation bar (Home, Services, About Us, Contact, Blog), and a "Book Now" button. Hero Section: Large banner image with a tagline like “A Vacation for Your Pets.” Features: Highlight unique services (e.g., Live Webcam, Specialized Care). Testimonials: Rotating carousel of customer reviews. Call to Action: A button leading to the booking system. Footer: Contact information, social media links, and a newsletter signup.

[Page 2 - Bookings]

Page 2: Booking Page Header: Logo and simplified navigation bar (Home, Services, Contact). Form Section: Dropdowns for pet type (Cat/Dog/Bunny). Calendar to select dates. Fields for pet details (name, special requirements). Service Packages: Display options (e.g., Standard, Premium). Dynamic Pricing: Update cost based on selected services. Footer: Payment gateway icons and a FAQ link.

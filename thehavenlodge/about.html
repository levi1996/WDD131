About Pet Haven Lodge

Your pet's home away from home since 2020

Our Story

Pet Haven Lodge was founded with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality care for pets while their owners are away. What started as a small family-operated business has grown into a premier pet care facility, known for its exceptional service and dedication to animal welfare.

Our facility spans over 5,000 square feet of indoor space and features a 2-acre outdoor play area, making it the perfect environment for pets to stay active and engaged during their stay with us.
